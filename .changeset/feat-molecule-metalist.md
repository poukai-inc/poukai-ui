Add `MetaList` molecule — semantic `<dl>` of label/value pairs for article meta, project spec panels, and pricing detail blocks. Supports `stacked` and `horizontal` orientations, optional hairline `dividers`, and consumer-controlled `labelWidth` for horizontal mode. Exported from the main barrel, `./molecules`, and `./molecules/MetaList` subpath.
